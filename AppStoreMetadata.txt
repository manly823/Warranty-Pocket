Description:
Warranty Pocket is your personal warranty vault that keeps every purchase protected and every receipt organized. Never lose a warranty again — scan receipts with your camera, track expiration dates, and get automatic reminders before coverage ends.

The app uses built-in OCR (Optical Character Recognition) powered by Apple's Vision framework to scan any receipt photo and automatically extract the store name, purchase date, and price. Simply point your camera at a receipt or select a photo from your library, and Warranty Pocket intelligently reads the text and pre-fills your warranty details. You can always edit and refine the extracted information manually for perfect accuracy.

Warranty Pocket comes pre-loaded with 12 sample warranties across multiple categories so you can immediately explore all features without entering any data first. From electronics like MacBook Pro and Apple Watch to furniture like Herman Miller chairs and IKEA desks, the sample data demonstrates how the app organizes and tracks real-world purchases with varying warranty durations.

Every warranty is tracked through its full lifecycle with color-coded status indicators. Active warranties show green shields, items expiring within 30 days display yellow warning triangles, and expired coverage shows red alerts. The animated countdown rings on each warranty card give you an instant visual sense of how much coverage time remains.

Smart push notifications ensure you never miss a warranty deadline. The app automatically schedules three reminders for each warranty: 30 days before expiration, 7 days before, and 1 day before. This gives you ample time to file any claims, request repairs, or plan replacement purchases.

The Insights dashboard provides a complete analytical overview of your warranty portfolio. View your total protected value, category breakdown with horizontal bar charts, value distribution across product types, an expiry timeline showing when warranties end over the next year, and a ranked list of your most valuable protected items.
