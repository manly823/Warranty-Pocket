Description:
Warranty Pocketio is your personal warranty vault that keeps every purchase protected and every receipt organized. Track expiration dates for all your warranties in one place and get automatic reminders before coverage ends. Whether you bought electronics, furniture, tools, or clothing, this app helps you stay on top of every warranty period.

Add warranties manually by entering the product name, store, purchase date, warranty duration, and price. You can also attach a receipt photo from your camera or photo library. The app includes optional text recognition that attempts to read receipt details and pre-fill fields for convenience. You can always edit and adjust any extracted information manually.

Warranty Pocketio comes pre-loaded with 12 sample warranties across multiple categories so you can immediately explore all features without entering any data first. The sample data covers electronics, furniture, appliances, tools, clothing, and automotive products with varying warranty durations, giving you a complete picture of how the tracking system works.

Every warranty is tracked through its full lifecycle with color-coded status indicators. Active warranties show green shields, items expiring within 30 days display yellow warning triangles, and expired coverage shows red alerts. The animated countdown rings on each warranty card give you an instant visual sense of how much coverage time remains.

The app schedules local push notification reminders for each warranty: 30 days before expiration, 7 days before, and 1 day before. This gives you ample time to file any claims, request repairs, or plan replacement purchases before your warranty window closes.

The Insights dashboard provides a complete analytical overview of your warranty portfolio. View your total protected value, category breakdown with horizontal bar charts, value distribution across product types, an expiry timeline showing when warranties end over the next year, and a ranked list of your most valuable protected items.

Organize your warranties into 9 categories: Electronics, Appliances, Furniture, Clothing, Automotive, Sports, Tools, Jewelry, and Other. Browse and filter by category, search by product or store name, and archive expired items to keep your active list clean. Each warranty stores the full detail including product name, store, purchase date, expiry date, price, notes, and optional receipt photo.

All data is stored locally on your device. No account creation required, no cloud sync, no data collection. Your warranty information stays private and secure on your phone.
